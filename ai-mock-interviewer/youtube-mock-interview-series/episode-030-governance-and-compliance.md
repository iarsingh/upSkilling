## Question 6

Interviewer:
What is the difference between ISO 27001 and NIST?

Pause the video and answer this question aloud.

Senior Associate answer:
ISO 27001 is an international standard focused on establishing and certifying an Information Security Management System (ISMS) - a formal, auditable management process with a certification outcome. NIST (particularly the Cybersecurity Framework and 800-series publications) is a US-originated, more flexible set of guidelines and control catalogs that organizations can adopt without formal certification, often used as a practical technical reference even by organizations pursuing ISO 27001 certification for the management process itself.

## Question 10

Interviewer:
How do you manage audit findings?

Pause the video and answer this question aloud.

Senior Associate answer:
I validate the finding, understand root cause, agree severity, assign an owner, define remediation actions, set due dates, collect evidence, and track closure. If remediation is delayed, I document risk acceptance or exception approval. I also look for systemic issues beyond the individual finding.
