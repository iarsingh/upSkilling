## Question 1

Interviewer:
Tell me about a difficult technology risk you managed.

Pause the video and answer this question aloud.

Senior Associate answer:
Use STAR. Example: "We had overprivileged service accounts across production projects. I assessed actual usage through audit logs, prioritized accounts with customer data access, partnered with engineering to create least-privilege roles, rolled changes gradually, monitored failures, and reported risk reduction. The result was lower privilege exposure without disrupting delivery."

Use this structure: direct answer -> real project example -> risk/tradeoff -> validation -> senior ownership point.
