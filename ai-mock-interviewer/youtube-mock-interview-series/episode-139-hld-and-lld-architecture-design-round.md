## Question 1

Experience level: 0-1 year | Foundation

Interviewer:
Explain the difference between high-level design and low-level design. Which decisions, diagrams, and review questions belong in each?

Pause the video and answer this question aloud.

Senior Associate answer guide:
Separate HLD from LLD explicitly. For HLD, clarify scale and quality attributes, define boundaries, components, data stores, integrations, and failure handling. For LLD, define responsibilities, interfaces, entities, state transitions, patterns, algorithms, exceptions, concurrency, extensibility, and tests. For this 0-1 year | Foundation question, the answer should demonstrate fundamental correctness and clear reasoning. Include a concrete example, the most important assumption or failure risk, and the evidence you would use to validate the conclusion.

Senior answer structure:
Use this structure: definition -> simple example -> basic validation.

Scenario-based practice:
Apply the question to a realistic product or business situation. Explain the context, data, method, tradeoff, validation, recommendation, and ownership for: Explain the difference between high-level design and low-level design. Which decisions, diagrams, and review questions belong in each?

What interviewer checks:
They are checking fundamental correctness and clear reasoning, plus whether you connect technical analysis to a defensible business decision.
