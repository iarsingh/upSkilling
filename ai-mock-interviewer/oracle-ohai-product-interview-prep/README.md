## Important AI-use rule

Oracle's guidelines allow online assistance for preparation, guidance, and brainstorming. During an interview, assessment, or live coding exercise, do not use AI, online search, or code-generation tools unless the interviewer explicitly permits it. Answers must accurately reflect your own experience and capabilities.

## Recommended answer structure

For technical answers: direct answer → architecture or algorithm → trade-offs → failure modes → validation → ownership.

For experience answers, use STAR:

- Situation: concise production context and business impact
- Task: your responsibility and success criteria
- Action: what you personally decided and implemented
- Result: quantified outcome, lessons, and follow-up improvements

Do not claim OCI, OpenShift, Kafka, or healthcare production experience unless you genuinely have it. Bridge honestly from comparable GCP/GKE, Pub/Sub, platform, or regulated-enterprise experience and explain how you would close the gap.
