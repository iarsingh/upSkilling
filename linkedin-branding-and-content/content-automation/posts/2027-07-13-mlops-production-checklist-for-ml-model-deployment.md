Want to understand Production checklist for ML model deployment? Build it small, break it deliberately, and observe the failure.

Topic: Production checklist for ML model deployment

Hands-on lab:
1. Create the smallest reproducible environment with one clear success condition.
2. Add versioning, approval gates, canary rollout, lineage, and rollback.
3. Inject one realistic failure and observe model quality, drift, latency, throughput, errors, and cost.
4. Write a short runbook covering diagnosis, mitigation, validation, and cleanup.

Production takeaway:
Good engineering makes the expected behavior observable, the risky change reversible, and the recovery path testable.
