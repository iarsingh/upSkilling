Automating the tedious parts of job hunting without automating away judgment.

Project: Job Application Bot — A Review-First Application Assistant
GitHub: https://github.com/iarsingh/upSkilling/tree/main/job-application-bot

What it does:
- Assists applications across LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, Uplers, and more
- Review-first design — nothing submits without a human check
- Browser automation paired with a config-driven profile

Why it matters:
High-volume applying without review produces noise; this keeps the volume while keeping a human in the loop on every submission.
