Want to understand Production checklist before deploying to Kubernetes? Build it small, break it deliberately, and observe the failure.

Topic: Production checklist before deploying to Kubernetes

Hands-on lab:
1. Create the smallest reproducible environment with one clear success condition.
2. Add requests and limits, probes, policies, progressive delivery, and rollback.
3. Inject one realistic failure and observe events, pod status, logs, metrics, endpoints, DNS, and node pressure.
4. Write a short runbook covering diagnosis, mitigation, validation, and cleanup.

Production takeaway:
Good engineering makes the expected behavior observable, the risky change reversible, and the recovery path testable.
