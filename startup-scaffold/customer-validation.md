# Customer Validation

## Goal

Before building too much, confirm whether DevOps/SRE/platform engineers feel this pain strongly enough.

## People To Interview

- DevOps engineers
- SREs
- platform engineers
- engineering managers
- startup CTOs
- ML platform engineers

## Interview Questions

Ask these:

1. How many production alerts do you get in a week?
2. What is the hardest part of incident debugging?
3. How do you currently write runbooks?
4. How do you summarize incidents after RCA?
5. What tools do you use for logs, metrics, and alerts?
6. Do junior engineers struggle during on-call?
7. What incident work feels repetitive?
8. Would an AI-assisted runbook generator help?
9. What would make you not trust such a tool?
10. Would you try a private demo?

## Strong Pain Signals

- "We waste time jumping between tools."
- "Runbooks are outdated."
- "Only senior engineers know how to debug."
- "RCA writing is painful."
- "Alerts lack context."
- "New joiners struggle during on-call."

## Validation Target

Before building full SaaS:

- 20 interviews
- 5 demo users
- 2 people willing to pay or pilot
- 1 company willing to share real anonymized incident examples
