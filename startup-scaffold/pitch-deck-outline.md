# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Title

CloudOps Copilot  
AI-assisted incident response for DevOps and platform teams.

## Slide 2: Problem

Incident response is slow because alerts lack context and debugging depends on senior engineers.

## Slide 3: Current Workflow

Engineers jump between:

- alerts
- dashboards
- logs
- Kubernetes events
- deployment history
- runbooks

## Slide 4: Solution

CloudOps Copilot turns incident context into:

- summary
- likely causes
- commands
- remediation
- runbook
- postmortem draft

## Slide 5: Target Customer

DevOps, SRE, platform, and ML platform teams running Kubernetes/cloud workloads.

## Slide 6: MVP

Paste alert/log/context -> get structured response.

## Slide 7: Differentiation

Focused on Kubernetes, GCP, Terraform, CI/CD, MLOps, and production reliability.

## Slide 8: Business Model

Consulting pilots first, SaaS later.

## Slide 9: Founder Fit

6.9+ years in DevOps, platform engineering, GCP, Kubernetes, Terraform, CI/CD, observability, and MLOps projects.

## Slide 10: Ask

Looking for pilot users, incident examples, and feedback from DevOps/SRE/platform teams.
