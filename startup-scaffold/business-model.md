# Business Model

## Path 1: Consulting First

Offer:

```text
I help DevOps and platform teams build AI-assisted incident response workflows, runbooks, and Kubernetes/GCP reliability automation.
```

Pricing:

- Audit: INR 25,000-50,000
- Setup project: INR 1-3 lakh
- Monthly support: INR 50,000+

## Path 2: SaaS

Pricing ideas:

- Free: 5 incident analyses/month
- Starter: $19/user/month
- Team: $99/team/month
- Enterprise: custom

## Path 3: Templates And Courses

Sell:

- Kubernetes incident runbook templates
- Terraform review checklist
- DevOps AI automation toolkit
- cohort/workshop for DevOps engineers

## First Revenue Goal

Do not wait for a full SaaS.

Target:

- 1 paid consulting pilot in 60 days
- 5 free MVP users
- 20 user interviews
