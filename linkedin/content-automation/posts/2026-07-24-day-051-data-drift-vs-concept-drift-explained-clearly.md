A practical checklist for Data drift vs concept drift explained clearly

Day 51/100 of my Data Science Series. This note is for data scientists and ML practitioners who want simple, production-minded ways to improve engineering systems.

- Define the production problem before choosing the tool or pattern.
- Check leakage, drift, missing values, metric choice, and business impact before trusting the model.
- Measure the result with one reliability metric and one delivery metric.
- Keep the implementation repeatable through automation and documentation.
