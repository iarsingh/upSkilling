## Daily Workflow

1. Open LinkedIn searches first and apply to fresh Easy Apply roles.
2. Open Naukri searches next and apply to high keyword-match roles.
3. Track every application immediately.
4. Send recruiter messages for the best 10-15 roles each day.
5. Follow up after 3 business days if no response.

## Minimum Match Rule

Apply when a role matches at least one of these:

- Direct role match: MLOps, DevOps, Platform, Cloud, GCP, Kubernetes, SRE, Infrastructure.
- At least 3 keyword matches from: GCP, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform, Ansible, CI/CD, GitOps, MLflow, Kubeflow, Vertex AI, Prometheus, Grafana, Python, Linux.
- Compensation likely supports 19 LPA or higher.

Skip when:

- Experience requirement is below 4 years or above 12 years.
- Role is mostly helpdesk, desktop support, manual testing, pure data analyst, or unrelated sales/pre-sales.
- Expected CTC cap is clearly below 19 LPA.
