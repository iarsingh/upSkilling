## Interview Questions and Answers

### 1. What is SRE?
SRE applies software engineering to operations, focusing on reliability, automation, measurement, and incident learning.

### 2. What is an error budget?
An error budget is the allowed unreliability over an SLO window. It helps balance feature velocity and reliability.

### 3. What is a blameless postmortem?
A blameless postmortem focuses on system improvement rather than individual blame.

### 4. What is MTTR?
Mean Time To Recovery measures how long it takes to restore service after an incident.

### 5. What should a good runbook include?
Symptoms, impact, dashboards, commands, mitigation steps, rollback steps, escalation contacts, and verification.
