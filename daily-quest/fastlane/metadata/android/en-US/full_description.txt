Daily Quest helps you keep momentum with three simple daily goals and a tiny reward game.

Use it to track a few useful habits, finish your daily checklist, build a streak, and play a fast 20-second tap challenge. Everything stays on your device, and the app does not use accounts, ads, analytics, or extra permissions.

Features:
- Three editable daily quests
- Daily reset and streak tracking
- Lightweight tap challenge with best score
- Local-only storage
- No sign-in required
- No ads or third-party SDKs
