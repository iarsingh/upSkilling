# Privacy Policy for Daily Quest

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Daily Quest is a simple checklist and tap game app.

## Data Collection

Daily Quest does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

## Local Storage

The app stores checklist labels, daily completion state, streak count, and best tap score locally on the user's device using Android shared preferences.

## Permissions

Daily Quest does not request camera, contacts, location, microphone, notification, or storage permissions.

## Third Parties

Daily Quest does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, or social login SDKs.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the email address listed on the Google Play store page.
