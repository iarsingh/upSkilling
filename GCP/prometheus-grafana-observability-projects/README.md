## Interview Questions and Answers

### 1. What are the three pillars of observability?
Metrics, logs, and traces. Modern observability also includes events, profiles, and user experience signals.

### 2. What is an SLI?
An SLI is a measured reliability signal, such as availability, latency, or error rate.

### 3. What is an SLO?
An SLO is a target for an SLI, such as 99.9% availability over 30 days.

### 4. What is Prometheus used for?
Prometheus collects time-series metrics, evaluates alert rules, and supports PromQL queries.

### 5. Why use OpenTelemetry?
OpenTelemetry provides vendor-neutral instrumentation for metrics, logs, and traces.
